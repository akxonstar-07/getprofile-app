2. **Crisp Minimalism**: A departure from the ornate designs of the past, Crisp Minimalism is all about simplicity, clean lines, and plenty of negative space. This aesthetic is perfect for creating a sense of calmness and focus.
3. **Subtle Brutalism**: As its name suggests, Subtle Brutalism takes the bold, industrial feel of traditional Brutalism and tones it down to create a more refined, sophisticated look. This trend adds an air of maturity and professionalism.
